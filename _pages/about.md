I am a Ph.D. student in the Psychology department at Seoul National University (SNU), Korea. I am a proud member of Human Factors Psychology Laboratory with an awesome advisor, Dr. Sowon Hahn. I am broadly interested in social cognition, empathy, social intelligence, AI, and psychology. In specific I am interested in integrating artificial intelligence with cognitive psychology to understand the core mechanisms of socially appropriate and desirable human-AI/robot collaboration. My Ph.D. work is supported by SNU Social Sciences R&D, Korean Ministry of SMEs and Startups, and KData.  

I have worked jointly with graduate students in Psychology, Industrial Engineering, Robotics, Data Science, and so on. I think I am more productive when working in teams (than working alone), so if you are interested in co-working, let’s talk! I am open to any new interdisciplinary research opportunities.  

Before joining HFPsych Lab, I completed a B.S. in Psychology from the University of Iowa (US). For more information, please check out my [CV](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1orivUnrDhg2bipcO70EO3DLfoSKWAsSwRY4wpJzILos/edit). 

I enjoy hiking (here is my favorite hiking picture in Bukhan mountain, Seoul), cooking, and blogging. I play piano in spare times when experiencing cognitive overload after long-hour works. Here is a photo of me playing digital piano my advisor gave us for a present!
